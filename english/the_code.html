Code number 1

A social care worker will help you with your rights. They will listen to what you want.

This means they will:work with you to help you live your life in the way you want. They will include you in all decisions about your life.

This means they will:respect what you want. They will listen to you and your carers.

This means they will:support your right to make all the decisions you can about your life.

This means they will:keep things as private as they can. They will explain if they need to share things with others.

This means they will:make sure you have the same chances as other people.

This means they will:make sure they do not treat you or your carers unfairly because of:
what you believe

This means they will:make sure they do not treat you or your carers unfairly because of:
what you prefer

This means they will:make sure they do not treat you or your carers unfairly because of:
your culture

This means they will:make sure they do not treat you or your carers unfairly because of:
the language that you speak

This means they will:make sure they do not treat you or your carers unfairly because of:
your way of life.

Code number 2

A social care worker will work for your trust and respect.

This means they will:be honest with you.

This means:write to you and talk with you in a way you can understand.

This means they will:make sure you and your carers can say things in the way you choose. And in the language you find easy.

This means they will:keep private things private. They will explain if they need to share some things with others.

This means they will:do what they say they will do, when they say they will do it.

This means they will:at times they may change what they have agreed to do. Then they will explain to you why.

This means they will:tell you about anything that makes it hard for them to work with you.

This means they will:not take presents or money from you or your carers. They are not allowed to.

Code number 3

A social care worker will help you to speak up and be safe.

This means they will:support you to be healthy and feel good about your life.

This means they will:support your carers to be healthy and to feel good about their life.

This means they will:support your right to speak up for yourself.

This means they will:support the right of your carers to speak up for themselves.

This means they will:be careful in work and keep you and themselves safe.

This means they will:report abuse. (Abuse is when someone hurts you or treats you badly.)

This means they will:report when you are treated in a way that is unfair or not safe.

This means they will:report anything around you that is not safe.

This means they will:report when staff work in a way that is not good or safe.

This means they will:support you and your carers to complain.

This means they will:take care not to take choices away from you.

Code number 4

A social care worker will respect your rights and help you be safe.

This means they will:know you have the right to take risks.

This means they will:support you to keep safe.

This means they will:look at what may happen with any risk you take.

This means they will:support you to be safe with any risk you take.

This means they will:write all this down. Give it to other staff who need to know about this.

Code number 5

A social care worker is trusted by everyone to do a good job.

This means they will:not hurt or abuse you, your carers or other workers.

This means they will:not treat you, your carers or other workers in an unfair way.

This means they will:not use what they know about you or your carers in a bad way.

This means they will:not behave with you or your carers in ways not good for you.

This means they will:not accept others treating you in an unfair way.

This means they will:not put you or other people at risk of being hurt.

This means they will:not behave badly, in work or out of work.

Code number 6

A social care worker will do a good job and get good training.

This means they will:keep the rules of their job and the law.

This means they will:write down what happens and what they are told.

This means they will:be honest when things go wrong.

This means they will:ask for help when they need it.

This means they will:work well with others.

This means they will:go on training to keep up to date.

This means they will:help to answer any questions if something has gone wrong.

Code number 7

A social care worker who is a boss will tell staff about these codes.

This means they will:make sure staff know how to use these codes.

This means they will:give staff teams time to talk about using these codes.

This means they will:give staff training to help them do their job better.

This means they will:meet with staff 1:1 to think about their job.

This means they will:listen to what people say about staff.

This means they will:tell them when they are doing a good job.

This means:when they are doing a bad job sort it out straight away.
